Omega 6 Fatty Acids are in the following foods and are inflammatory.

-   poultry
-   nuts
-   vegetable oil
-   grains

Should have a 1:1 balance with [omega 3]({{< relref "20231004224451-omega_3.md" >}}) fatty acids, though American diets are wildly out of balance.
